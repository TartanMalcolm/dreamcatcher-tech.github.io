Roadmap

The roadmap lays out the NFAs that our efforts go towards inducing.

An NFA should have these properties:

  1. Title
  2. Description
  3. Deliverable Format
  4. Bid Price
  5. Bid Due Date
  6. Bid Attribution split
  7. QA authority
  8. Detailed requirements
  9. Dependencies
